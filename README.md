### 🗺️ OGPS Reports · *Contributor*
Collaborative urban incident reporting platform. Citizens can report potholes, broken streetlights and other issues with geotagged photos. Includes interactive map, community validation and incident status tracking. Versioned up to v1.1.3.

`Kotlin` `Spring Boot` `PostgreSQL` `React` `Vite` `Node.js`

- 🔗 [Backend](https://github.com/Osdan-flo/OGPS-REPORTS)
- 🔗 [Frontend](https://github.com/Osdan-flo/ogps-reports-frontend)

### 🏠 Hommunity · *Contributor*
Mobile access control system for residential and private areas. Residents generate QR codes for visitors with configurable time limits. The doorman scans the QR to authorize entry. Versioned up to v1.0.1.

`Java` `Spring Boot` `PostgreSQL` `Kotlin` `Android Studio`

> Private repository — pending publication.
